Frequently asked questions

Does this replace our curriculum?

No. Oak Homeschooling Planner is not a curriculum — it's a planning and organisation layer that sits on top of great curriculum sources. You choose the content; we help you schedule, track, and manage it.

Are you affiliated with Oak National Academy?

No. Oak Homeschooling Planner is fully independent and has no affiliation, endorsement, or formal relationship with Oak National Academy. Oak National Academy is a government-funded provider of free, high-quality teaching resources for teachers and pupils. We are grateful for the excellent resources they make publicly available, and any integration is made solely through their publicly available materials. Visit thenational.academy to learn more about their work.

Which content providers are you integrated with?

We currently integrate with Oak National Academy's publicly available resources and a growing library of past exam-board papers. You can also add your own custom resources and links to any lesson.

Is this suitable for all ages and subjects?

Oak Homeschooling Planner is designed for students aged 11–18 and covers a wide range of subjects aligned with the national curriculum. The scheduling system is flexible enough to accommodate different paces and subject combinations.

How does the progress record feature work?

Every completed lesson is logged with a timestamp, time spent, and quiz scores. This builds a running record of your child's education that you can review at any time, export, or share with anyone who needs to see it.

Can I customise the schedule for holidays or a different pace?

Absolutely. You can set term dates, mark holiday periods, adjust the weekly lesson load, and change the pace at any time. The platform recalculates your schedule automatically.

What happens after the free trial?

After your 14-day free trial, you can continue on the free plan with basic features, or upgrade to Individual or Family for full access. No credit card is required to start, and you won't be charged unless you choose to upgrade.

Is there support if I get stuck?

Yes. We offer email support for all users and priority support for Family plan subscribers. We also have a growing library of guides and walkthroughs to help you get the most from the platform.

Do students need their own login?

Yes. Each student gets their own login so they can see their personal daily agenda, track their own progress, and earn badges independently. Parents have a separate dashboard with an overview of all their children.

How does this help if a local authority contacts us?

Being well-organised means that if a local authority makes contact, you can respond confidently and professionally. Your records are clear, up to date, and easy to share. Many of our parents have found that good preparation turns what felt like a stressful prospect into a straightforward and even supportive conversation.
